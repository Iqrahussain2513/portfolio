Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my design services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple logo design typically takes 1-2 weeks, while a complete website redesign can take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in the pricing?

All packages include source files, multiple revisions, and ongoing support. The specific deliverables depend on the package chosen. I'll provide a detailed breakdown of what's included for your specific project.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and use various tools to ensure smooth communication and project delivery regardless of location.

Can you help with existing projects?

Absolutely! I can help redesign existing projects, improve user experience, or add new features. I'll analyze your current setup and provide recommendations for improvements.

What design tools do you use?

I use industry-standard tools including Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and various prototyping tools. I'm flexible with tool preferences and can adapt to your team's workflow.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, all packages include post-delivery support. The duration varies by package, and I also offer ongoing maintenance and update services for long-term clients.
